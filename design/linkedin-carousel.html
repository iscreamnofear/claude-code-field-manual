From the Guide
5 rules that make Claude Code actually deliver
1
Start with the outcome, not the method
"I need a sales dashboard" — not "Build me a React app."
2
Describe your audience
Claude makes different decisions for your boss vs. your customers.
3
List what you need, specifically
Not "make it look good." Try "3 sections, brand colors, CTA above the fold."
4
Say what you DON'T want
Claude is enthusiastic. Tell it to keep things minimal.
5
Always ask for a plan first
"Before building anything, explain your plan." This one habit prevents most bad outcomes.
